# Compliance Model

This repository treats compliance as a first-class product concern while keeping all flows simulated.

## What The Prototype Models

- KYC status before purchase.
- Jurisdiction allowlists per asset.
- Maximum tokens per investor.
- Minimum order size.
- Available token supply.
- Asset-level risk notes and disclosure documents.

## What It Does Not Do

- Legal entity formation.
- Securities registration or exemption handling.
- Broker-dealer, ATS, transfer agent, or custodian workflows.
- Real KYC/AML checks.
- Accreditation verification.
- Sanctions screening.
- Fiat or crypto settlement.
- Tax reporting.

## Product Principle

The UI should never imply that a token balance alone creates enforceable real-world ownership. A real implementation needs legal agreements, issuer records, custody controls, investor disclosures, transfer restrictions, and jurisdiction-specific regulatory review.
